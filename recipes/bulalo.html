Bulalo

Bulalo is a beef dish from the Philippines.

It is a light colored soup that is made by cooking beef shanks and bone marrow until the collagen and fat has been melted into the clear broth. It typically includes leafy vegetables, corn on the cob, scallions, onions, garlic, ginger, and fish sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef shank
  • 1/2 cabbage
  • 1 bundle bok choy
  • 2 cobs corn
  • 2 tablespoons of whole peppercorn
  • 1 onion
  • 34 ounces of water
  • fish sauce to taste

Steps

  1. In a big cooking pot, pour in water and bring to a boil
  2. Put-in the beef shank followed by the onion and whole pepper corn then simmer for 1.5 hours (30 mins if using a pressure cooker) or until meat is tender.
  3. Add the corn and simmer for another 10 minutes
  4. Add the fish sauce, cabbage, pechay, and green onion (onion leeks)
  5. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
